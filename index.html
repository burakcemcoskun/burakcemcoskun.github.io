Burak Cem Coşkun

Anatolian Phusiologos • Physicist • Poet • Writer • Rebel of Science

About

Born in Istanbul in 1994, Burak Cem Coşkun graduated with distinction from the Physics Engineering Department of Istanbul Technical University in 2017. He conducted research on neutron stars and nuclear physics at the Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) in Sweden. He pursued master’s studies in theoretical astrophysics at the University of Bonn and continued graduate studies at Sabancı University. His work focuses on ultra-dense neutron superfluids, exotic nuclei, and high-energy astrophysics.

Academic Identity
